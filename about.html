About Great Tables

Great Tables is a small tutorial site focused on one thing: practical, step-by-step Google Sheets guides. No account required, no unnecessary padding — just the formula or feature you're looking for, explained clearly with a working example.

Why This Site Exists

This project started as a hands-on way to learn SEO — researching real search questions, writing content that actually answers them, and publishing it the same way a real small business or independent blogger would. Every tutorial here is tested in an actual Google Sheets file before being written up, rather than copied or paraphrased from other sources.

How Tutorials Are Written

  • Each topic starts from a real question people search for.
  • Steps are tested directly in Google Sheets, not assumed from memory.
  • Formulas are shown exactly as they appear in the formula bar, not simplified to the point of being wrong.
  • Articles are updated when Google Sheets changes how a feature works.

Contact

Spotted an error, an outdated step, or have a tutorial request? Reach out — feedback directly improves the accuracy of this site.
